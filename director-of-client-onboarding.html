Director of Client Onboarding | Jobs at SmartLogic

Background: SmartLogic is a profitable, growing web/mobile product development company. We have a great reputation and a great team. We’re looking to grow. We’ve worked hard to institute scalable project management processes and now we need to institute scalable sales processes. We have an influx of inbound leads that need to be turned into deals. As we grow the team, we expect we’ll need to convert even more leads into clients to continue to fuel our growth.

We’re looking for a developer with experience in the sales process. While part of this role will be development and programming, the primary responsibilities of this position are to (1) convert leads into deals and (2) ensure the smoothest possible handoff for new clients as they get worked into our process.

Expect that in an average week you will spend 30 hours on web/mobile development and 10 hours on sales-related activities. As the company grows and as our marketing activities increase, you will spend more time on sales-related activities.

High-Level Goals

  1. Ensure that leads go through the sales funnel as quickly as possible
  2. Increase lead-to-deal ratio
  3. Identify and contact prospects
  4. Increase the number of events at which SmartLogic is represented. This will include technical meetups, conferences, etc.

Primary Objectives

  1. Work with Yair (SmartLogic’s President) to create methods for increasing visibility into the sales funnel
  2. Work with Dan (our Director of Development Operations aka DODO) to identify the smoothest possible onboarding process, i.e. handoff between your responsibilities and his
  3. Qualify, respond to, and close inbound leads
  4. Remove Yair from the proposal writing process
  5. Do research to generate targeted leads
  6. Fill the rest of your time with design and/or development work
  7. Attend technical meetups, especially those sponsored by SmartLogic
  8. Attend technical conferences
  9. Represent SmartLogic at events we sponsor

Daily and Weekly Responsibilities; Tactics

  1. Build Processes
    1. Create a method to manage the sales funnel and give Yair and Dan visibility
    2. Work with Dan and the team to build processes that allow the smoothest, most efficient, most scalable onboarding process
    3. Create checklists or any other tactical processes as necessary
  2. Lead-to-Deal
    1. Set up conference calls between yourself, inbound leads, and Yair
    2. Involve other team members as necessary to help win business
    3. Involve other team members as necessary to create estimates and proposals
    4. Write proposals or complete any other tasks necessary to close a deal
    5. Prepare MSA’s and SOW’s for Yair’s approval
    6. Document anything you may say about timeline and budget, and anything a prospect may say about timeline and budget
  3. After the Deal
    1. Transfer any important information to the rest of the team so that we have everything we need to be successful in the project
    2. Attend kickoff and epic meetings
    3. Stay involved as long as necessary: until Dan / DODO is fully ramped up and in control

Who’s a Good Fit for this Job

We’re looking for a developer who has been involved in the sales process in some regard. For example: you may have been pulled into sales calls or you may have been involved in writing proposals. Whatever the case, you need to have a strong interest in turning leads into deals (as an aside, isn’t it interesting that those two words are anagrams?).

You’ll need to be smart, independent, and ambitious to fit in here. We’re looking for someone who wants to own the onboarding process and materially impact how we do business.
