The Newseum

To commemorate the 10th anniversary of 9/11, The Newseum asked SmartLogic to build a Flash-based application that captures and displays messages from their visitors. The application is currently on display at The Newseum and allows visitors to share their messages by writing directly on the kiosk screen with a stylus, as well as browse messages left by others.
